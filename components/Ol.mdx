# Ol

Use Ol for ordered lists. There is not a special component for `li`, simply use a plain `li` html tag.

## Examples

<Playground>
  <Text>The first three days of the week are:</Text>
  <Ol>
    <li>Monday</li>
    <li>Tuesday</li>
    <li>Wednesday</li>
  </Ol>
  <Text>What is your favorite day?</Text>
</Playground>
